# My PRRT2 — Nav Channel & Symptom Map

**One PRRT2 patient's full symptom constellation mapped to the underlying circuit mechanisms — from Nav channel biology through basal ganglia dysfunction to each specific presentation.**

**🧭 PRRT2.org Perspective**

_This is a personal document — one patient's experience of living with a confirmed PRRT2 pathogenic variant, diagnosed in adulthood after decades without a name for it. The symptom descriptions and circuit connections are grounded in the published science. The compensation strategy in Section 7 reflects one individual's supervised medical management — it is not a recommendation or a protocol to follow. Nothing here is medical advice._
{% endhint %}

This diagram was built to answer a question most PRRT2 patients never get answered clearly: *why* does this gene produce this particular combination of symptoms in this particular person? The circuit-level explanation — following the chain from Nav channel instability through basal ganglia misfiring to each named presentation — is both scientifically grounded and, for many patients, the first coherent account of their own neurology they have ever seen.
